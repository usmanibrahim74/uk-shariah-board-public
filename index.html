Introduction

About UK Shariah Board

The UK Shariah Board is a leading Islamic legal advisory organization dedicated to providing comprehensive Shariah-compliant solutions for individuals and families across the United Kingdom. Our mission is to ensure that Muslim communities have access to authentic Islamic legal guidance in matters of personal law.

With a team of qualified Islamic scholars and legal experts, we specialize in addressing critical life matters including Marriage, Divorce, Khula (judicial divorce), Wirasat (Islamic inheritance), and Wills. Our services are designed to bridge the gap between Islamic principles and UK legal systems.

We operate through a network of central and zonal offices, ensuring that our services are accessible to communities throughout the UK. Our commitment is to uphold the highest standards of Islamic jurisprudence while providing practical, compassionate guidance to those in need.

Process Flow

Our streamlined process ensures efficient and transparent handling of your legal matters.

1

Initial Consultation

Contact us through our website, phone, or visit one of our offices. Our team will schedule a consultation to understand your requirements.

2

Application Submission

Complete and submit the required application form along with necessary documentation. Our team will review your application.

3

Review by Shariah Board

Your case is reviewed by our qualified Shariah scholars and legal experts to ensure proper Islamic jurisprudence is applied.

4

Approval and Documentation

Once approved, all necessary documentation is prepared and certified according to Shariah principles and UK legal requirements.

5

Completion and Follow-up

Receive your completed documents and certificates. Our team provides ongoing support and guidance as needed.
